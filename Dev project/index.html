My experience as a homeschool mother, curriculum developer, and teacher equips me to create inclusive and impactful educational content. Furthermore, my upbringing as a member of an underserved community equips me with a heart to serve the underserved with compassion. As I strive to earn a P.H.D. in organizational leadership, I am deeply committed to enhancing the effectiveness of those committed to serving others.

ImpactAlly

ImpactAlly is a resource center uniquely designed to address a critical challenge encountered by organizations dedicated to serving underserved communities: the necessity of specialized training and development. Our project will equip service-oriented teams with the tools and knowledge essential for delivering impactful and compassionate service. I recognize that effectiveness in service is not just about expertise but also about understanding the unique challenges, areas of need, and the importance of empathy in the communities we serve. By providing comprehensive training and development, ImpactAlly ensures that teams are well-informed and deeply connected to the people and the causes they serve. This initiative will transform the landscape of community service, ensuring that those often overlooked receive the highest quality of care and support. ImpactAlly is not just about building skills; it's about fostering a heart of service, where knowledge meets compassion, leading to a more effective and empathetic approach to serving the underserved.
